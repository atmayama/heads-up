How to play 🎮

1️⃣
Pick a category

Choose from Animals, Movies, Celebrities, and more.

📱
Hold the phone on your forehead

Face the screen away from you. Your friends can see the word, you can't!

🗣️
Friends give you clues

They describe, act, or make sounds — anything to help you guess the word.

✅
Tilt to answer

Tilt forward (or tap ✓) = Got it!
Tilt back (or tap ✗) = Pass.
On desktop: use ↑ / ↓ arrow keys.

⏱️
Beat the clock

Get as many correct as you can before 60 seconds runs out!
